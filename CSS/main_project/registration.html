About the Event

DeSci Mania at Zo is a 48-hour event where developers, designers, and innovators come together to create amazing projects. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, there's a place for you!

DeSci Mania: A month-long hybrid hackathon experience aimed at catalyzing innovation in the Decentralised Science (DeSci) ecosystem. This event leverages Zo Houses as IRL nodes to create a unique phygital hackathon experience.

TEAM DESCRIPTION

Zo World is a cultural network state blending tech, real estate, and innovation. With over 12 years of expertise, Zo World curates ecosystems for inspiration and accelerated learning. Our Zo Houses function as decentralised nodes, fostering the Web3 ethos of collaboration and creativity.

Our team includes seasoned leaders in the Web3 and culture domains, along with a network of 490+ founder members & over 167k+ citizens. Zo Houses in Bangalore (BLRxZo) and San Francisco (SFOxZo) serve as the perfect venues for cross-continental collaboration and inspiration.

Hackathon DESCRIPTION

DeSci Mania is a month-long hybrid hackathon designed to accelerate innovation in the DeSci ecosystem. By combining online collaboration with physical co-working spaces at Zo Houses in Bangalore and San Francisco, the event provides a platform for builders, researchers, and innovators to explore decentralised approaches to science.
